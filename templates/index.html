Common Types of Cyber Crime

Financial Fraud

Scams involving online banking, credit cards, UPI fraud, and fake investment schemes.

Identity Theft

Misuse of personal information to impersonate someone and commit fraudulent activities.

Cyberstalking & Bullying

Harassment, threats, and intimidation carried out using digital communication platforms.

Malware & Ransomware

Attacks where malicious software is used to damage or seize control of your computer systems.

Simple Steps to Report a Cyber Crime

1

Gather Evidence

Collect all relevant details like screenshots, transaction IDs, emails, and URLs.

2

File a Complaint

Use our portal to fill out the complaint form with all the collected information.

3

Get a Complaint ID

Receive an official ID to track the status of your complaint and for future reference.

4

Follow Up

Our team will review your case and may contact you for more details if needed.
